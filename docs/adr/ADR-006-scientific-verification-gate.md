# ADR-006: Scientific Verification Mandatory Before Content Generation

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Medical content carries patient and compliance risk if produced from unverified or low-quality sources.

## Decision
Content generation is allowed only from approved knowledge units tied to verified references and auditable review actions.

## Consequences
- Strong governance and traceability.
- Additional review latency is accepted for safety and quality.
- Generation services must enforce source tracing metadata.
